Apple Chips

Description>

This is a recipe for making apple chips!

Ingredients

  • 2 apples
  • 1 1/2 tsp white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 225 degrees F
  2. Slice apples and place them on the baking sheet
  3. Mix sugar and cinnamon together; sprinkle over apple slices
  4. Bake until the apples are dried up and the edges curl up
  5. Take them out with a metal spatula until they are cool and crispy
